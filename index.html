About Us

Step into a haven of timeless grooming traditions blended seamlessly with contemporary flair. At Mikes, we invite you to experience the artistry of barbering in an ambiance that echoes both the nostalgia of yesteryears and the vibrancy of modern trends. Our freshly minted establishment pays homage to the golden era of barbershops, where every visit was an experience to cherish. Nestled in the heart of Chapel Hill, our cozy space is designed to whisk you away from the hustle and bustle of daily life, immersing you in an oasis of relaxation and rejuvenation.
